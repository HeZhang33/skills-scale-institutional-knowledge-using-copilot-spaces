# Role Alignment Checklist

## Purpose
Ensure all team members understand their roles, responsibilities, and how they interact with others during onboarding or project kickoffs. This checklist helps establish clear expectations and communication paths from day one.

## When to Use
- New team member onboarding
- Project kickoff meetings
- Team reorganization or role changes
- Cross-functional project initiation
- Quarterly team alignment reviews

---

## General Role Clarity

- [ ] **Role Definition**: Each team member has reviewed their role description in the [Roles and Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) document
- [ ] **Responsibilities**: Primary responsibilities are clearly understood and documented
- [ ] **Goals**: Individual and role-specific goals are defined and align with team objectives
- [ ] **Decision Authority**: Clear understanding of what decisions can be made independently vs. requiring consultation
- [ ] **Success Metrics**: Know how role performance will be measured and evaluated

---

## Communication Paths

- [ ] **Primary Contacts**: Identified key contacts for each role (who to go to for what)
- [ ] **Meeting Cadence**: Understand which meetings are required vs. optional
- [ ] **Communication Channels**: Know where to communicate (Slack channels, email lists, project boards)
- [ ] **Escalation Path**: Clear process for escalating issues or blockers
- [ ] **Status Updates**: Understand format and frequency of expected status updates

---

## Cross-Role Dependencies

- [ ] **Upstream Dependencies**: Identified roles that provide inputs/deliverables to your work
- [ ] **Downstream Dependencies**: Identified roles that depend on your outputs/deliverables
- [ ] **Handoff Points**: Clear understanding of when and how work is handed off between roles
- [ ] **Collaboration Touchpoints**: Regular sync points with dependent roles are scheduled
- [ ] **Feedback Loops**: Mechanisms in place for giving and receiving feedback across roles

---

## Project-Specific Alignment

- [ ] **Project Goals**: Understand how your role contributes to specific project outcomes
- [ ] **Timeline & Milestones**: Aware of project timeline and key milestone dependencies
- [ ] **Risk Areas**: Identified potential risks related to your role and mitigation strategies
- [ ] **Resource Allocation**: Understand time commitment and availability expectations
- [ ] **Definition of Done**: Clear criteria for what "done" means for deliverables from your role

---

## Tools & Access

- [ ] **Tool Access**: Have necessary access to all required tools and systems
- [ ] **Documentation**: Know where to find project documentation and how to contribute
- [ ] **Repositories**: Access to code repositories, design files, or other key resources
- [ ] **Credentials**: Obtained all necessary credentials and permissions
- [ ] **Training**: Completed required training for tools or processes specific to the role

---

## Role-Specific Checklists

### For Developers
- [ ] Code repository access and development environment setup complete
- [ ] Understand branching strategy and code review process
- [ ] Familiar with testing requirements and CI/CD pipeline
- [ ] Know how to raise technical concerns or propose architectural changes

### For Product Managers
- [ ] Access to product analytics and user research tools
- [ ] Understand backlog prioritization criteria and process
- [ ] Identified key stakeholders and their expectations
- [ ] Established cadence for roadmap reviews and updates

### For Project Managers
- [ ] Project plan created and baselined with team agreement
- [ ] Risk register initialized and review cadence set
- [ ] Stakeholder communication plan documented
- [ ] Status reporting format and frequency agreed upon

### For Scrum Masters
- [ ] Sprint ceremonies scheduled and attendees confirmed
- [ ] Team working agreements documented and agreed upon
- [ ] Metrics tracking tools configured (burndown, velocity, etc.)
- [ ] Retrospective format and action item tracking established

### For UX Designers
- [ ] Access to design tools and component libraries
- [ ] Understand design review process and approval workflow
- [ ] User research plan aligned with product priorities
- [ ] Design handoff process established with developers

### For Business Analysts
- [ ] Requirements gathering approach agreed with stakeholders
- [ ] User story template and acceptance criteria format established
- [ ] Access to business process documentation and data sources
- [ ] Requirements review and sign-off process defined

### For Technical Writers
- [ ] Documentation repository access and publishing workflow understood
- [ ] Style guide and templates available and reviewed
- [ ] Documentation roadmap aligned with product releases
- [ ] Content review process established with SMEs

---

## Sign-off

Once all relevant items are checked:

- [ ] **Team Member Acknowledgment**: I have reviewed and understand my role, responsibilities, and interactions
- [ ] **Manager Approval**: Manager has reviewed and confirmed role alignment
- [ ] **Date Completed**: _______________
